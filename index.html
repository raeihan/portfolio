Photography is the process of painting/writing using
light medium. As a general term, photography means process
or a method for producing images or photos of an object
by recording the reflection of light hitting the object on
light sensitive media.

Illustration

Illustrations are the result of visualization of writing
using drawing, painting, photography or other fine arts
techniques that emphasize the relationship of the subject
to the writing in question rather than the form.
